# Investment Analysis Projects

This repository contains a collection of independent investment, equity, and macroeconomic analysis projects implemented in Python and Jupyter notebooks. The work emphasizes data-driven reasoning, clear visualization, and careful interpretation of financial and economic relationships.

## Projects

### Portfolio Performance & Risk Analysis
Time-weighted performance analysis of a personal equity portfolio, including return attribution, volatility, drawdowns, and benchmark comparison.

➡️ [`portfolio-performance/`](portfolio-performance)

---

### Mortgage Rate & Housing Market Analysis
Statistical analysis and visualization of U.S. mortgage rates and housing-related macroeconomic indicators, including time-series transformations and regression analysis.

➡️ [`mortgage-rate-analysis/`](mortgage-rate-analysis)

---

### Earnings Surprises & Share Price Reactions
A pilot event study examining how quarterly earnings surprises relate to short-term share price reactions, with a focus on asymmetry between earnings beats and misses. The initial analysis uses Refinitiv IBES data for Walmart (WMT) as a case study.

➡️ [`WMT Earnings Beats & Misses Analysis/`](WMT Earnings Beats & Misses Analysis)

## Tools & Methods
- Python (pandas, NumPy, matplotlib, statsmodels)
- Jupyter notebooks
- Time-series analysis and visualization
- Financial performance and event-study methodologies

## Notes
All projects are for educational and illustrative purposes only and do not constitute investment advice.
